After completing my BSc in CSIT, I began my career as a Software Engineer. While working, I gradually transitioned into the role of a Research Engineer. Although research was something I deeply wanted to pursue, I quickly realized I wasn’t yet a qualified researcher. There was a clear gap in my knowledge and skills that I needed to bridge.

My growing passion for AI ultimately led me to join the MTech in Artificial Intelligence program. I chose this degree to build strong foundational knowledge in the field and transform myself into a capable, professional researcher.

My goal with this Master’s is to develop the expertise required to publish influential papers and make meaningful contributions to the field of AI, contributions that ultimately improve people’s lives.
